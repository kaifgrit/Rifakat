Meet Mr. Salim Khan

With over 7 years of experience in the footwear industry, Mr. Salim Khan founded Rifakat Shoe Garden in Nuh, Haryana, with a vision to bring quality, style, and comfort to every customer.

"At Rifakat Shoe Garden, we believe that the right pair of shoes can transform not just your outfit, but your entire day. Every shoe we offer is carefully selected to ensure it meets our standards of quality, durability, and style."

From top brands like Nike and Adidas to elegant formal shoes and sandals, we curate collections that blend timeless style with modern trends. We also specialize in high-quality brand first copies at the cheapest prices, ensuring you get the look you want for less. Visit us today and experience personalized service that puts your comfort first.
